# Mobile Turtle Bot: Autonomous Navigation with SLAM

A robotics project showcasing the development of a mobile robot using **Dijkstra's algorithm** for pathfinding and **SLAM** (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) for autonomous navigation in unknown environments.
