# Garage@EEE: Where Innovation Meets Community

Garage@EEE is a student-led maker space in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering. We provide the environment, materials, and funding for students to develop their ideas, alongside their technical skills. Furthermore, we run creative initiatives to provide opportunities for our ambassadors to enhance other holistic skills.

![NTU EEE Experience](/assets/img/dy04/gallery/ntueee.jpeg)
_My journey at NTU's School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering - where innovation begins_

## My Experience

Garage@EEE was honestly one of the best parts of my NTU journey. It's a makerspace where we're free to build, tinker, and explore whatever ideas we have ‚Äî from electronics to software, **no limits**.

### What Makes Garage Special

The space is equipped with everything an engineer could dream of:
- **Soldering stations** for precision electronics work
- **3D printers** for rapid prototyping
- **Drill presses** and mechanical tools
- **Raspberry Pi & ESP32** development boards
- **Arduino ecosystem** for embedded projects

### My Role: Training and Development Committee

I joined as part of the **Training and Development Committee**, where I got hands-on experience with:

#### Hardware Projects
- **Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs)**: Designing and assembling custom circuits
- **3D Printing**: Rapid prototyping for mechanical components  
- **Embedded Systems**: Programming microcontrollers for IoT applications
- **Electronics Integration**: Combining sensors, actuators, and control systems

#### Teaching & Knowledge Sharing
One of the most rewarding aspects was running **projectshops** where I taught:
- **ReactJS**: Modern web development frameworks
- **NodeJS**: Backend development and API creation
- **JavaScript**: Core programming concepts and applications

**Audience**: IEM (Industrial & Systems Engineering) and CS (Computer Science) students

It was super rewarding to share what I knew and learn alongside others. Teaching reinforced my own understanding while helping others start their coding journeys.
