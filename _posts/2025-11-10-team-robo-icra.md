## About the ICRA BARN Challenge

The **BARN (Benchmark Autonomous Robot Navigation)** Challenge is part of the IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), one of the most prestigious robotics conferences worldwide. The challenge focuses on autonomous robot navigation in cluttered, dynamic environments that simulate real-world scenarios.
